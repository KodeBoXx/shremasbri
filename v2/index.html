About Us

Built on Partnership,
Driven by Purpose

Shrem Asbri BG Limited (SABL) is a joint venture between Shrem Group and Asbri Energy Pvt. Ltd., focused on developing and operating large-scale Compressed Bio-Gas (CBG) plants across India. The company converts agricultural and organic waste into clean, renewable fuel.

Combining Asbri's technical and EPC expertise with Shrem's financial and infrastructure strengths, SABL operates as an integrated player across the entire CBG value chain.

What We Do

Across the Full CBG Value Chain

From project development and investment to long-term operations — SABL combines institutional capital with technology-led execution across every stage of a Compressed Bio-Gas plant's lifecycle.

CBG Plant Development

End-to-end development of Compressed Bio-Gas plants — from feasibility and site selection to regulatory approvals, financing close and project commissioning.

Investment & Project Development

Structuring long-horizon capital for CBG infrastructure, with institutional backing from the Shrem group and a pipeline of bankable projects across key Indian states.

Plant Operations & Maintenance

Long-term operations, performance monitoring and maintenance of CBG plants for optimal output, safety compliance and maximum asset longevity.

Green Energy Supply

Supply of purified, compressed biogas to industrial, commercial and transport off-takers — enabling businesses to meet sustainability targets with reliable renewable fuel.
