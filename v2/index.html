01 / About

Built on Partnership,
Driven by Purpose

Shrem Esbri BG Limited is a joint venture formed between Shrem Group — a leading investment platform — and Esbri Tech, a Chennai-based EPC specialist with over a decade of proven experience in compressed biogas infrastructure.

Together, we develop and operate CBG plants across India, transforming agricultural and organic waste into clean, affordable energy. Our combined strengths in institutional finance and engineering excellence position us uniquely in India's rapidly growing green energy sector.

EPC-grade project execution with a proven track record
Fully aligned with the Government of India's SATAT Scheme
Institutional investment backing and capital strength
Pan-India development pipeline spanning 5+ states
